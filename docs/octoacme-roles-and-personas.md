# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designers

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure the product is usable, accessible, and valuable to end users through research, interaction design, and usability validation.

### Responsibilities
- Own user research, usability testing, and accessibility audits
- Develop wireframes, prototypes, and design handoffs
- Collaborate with product managers and developers on workflows and requirements

### Goals
- Deliver thoughtful, accessible, validated user experiences
- Reduce customer friction and support needs
- Advocate for data-driven and inclusive design practices

### Typical Communication
- Share research insights and usability findings with the team
- Participate in design reviews and specification workshops
- Collaborate on problem framing and solution brainstorming

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Works closely with Developers for design validation and handoff
- Collaborates with Product Managers to refine feature scope based on user feedback and research
- Coordinates with QA for accessibility and usability checks

---

## DevOps Engineers

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and operate the infrastructure, pipelines, and monitoring systems that ensure reliable delivery and scalability.

### Responsibilities
- Implement and maintain CI/CD pipelines and infrastructure-as-code
- Monitor system health, set up alerts and dashboards
- Support release and rollback, incident management and postmortems

### Goals
- Speed up, automate, and secure delivery pipelines
- Minimize downtime and manual ops work
- Increase system reliability and observability

### Typical Communication
- Release and deployment coordination with Developers and PMs
- Incident reports and operational metrics sharing
- Infrastructure and automation design reviews

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Enable Developers to ship and recover quickly via stable infrastructure
- Work with Project Managers and QA on release planning and incident escalation/resolution

---

## Customer Support

### Role Summary
Customer Support acts as the bridge between users and the engineering team, ensuring customer needs and issues are surfaced, tracked, and resolved.

### Responsibilities
- Provide user-facing support and triage reported issues
- Gather and synthesize feedback and trends
- Facilitate communication between users, developers, and PMs

### Goals
- Speedy and clear responses to user queries and incidents
- Reduce repetitive support burdens via documentation and process improvement
- Advocate for customer voice in roadmap planning

### Typical Communication
- User reports, bug triage, and support ticket reviews
- Feedback sharing in sprint reviews and planning
- Partnership with QA for reproduction of user issues

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Collaborates with Product Managers to prioritize bug fixes and improvements
- Informs Developers and QA on real-world issue reproduction and solutions
- Supports Project Managers with user communications during releases and incidents

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
The Security Lead defines and maintains secure practices, monitors threats, and ensures compliance throughout development and operations.

### Responsibilities
- Lead threat modeling, security reviews, and vulnerability management
- Oversee compliance with security and privacy standards
- Conduct security incident response and postmortem analysis

### Goals
- Reduce vulnerabilities shipped to production
- Raise team awareness of security and privacy best practices
- Rapid and coordinated incident response

### Typical Communication
- Security check-ins and review documentation
- Coordination with Developers during feature design/implementation
- Incident response briefings and compliance status updates

### Interactions with Existing Roles
- Works with DevOps on vulnerability scanning and incident playbooks
- Reviews new designs and features with Developers and Product Managers
- Advises Project Managers on risk registers and stakeholder comms for security issues

## How these personas are used in the exercise

- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.

---

## Checklist: Role Assignment & Coverage

- [ ] All required core and supporting roles are assigned for the project (see list above).
- [ ] Each role has a named owner and alternates are identified for out-of-office coverage.
- [ ] Role responsibilities and communication paths are reviewed during project kickoff.
- [ ] Interactions and boundaries between roles are clear to all team members.
- [ ] For new or unassigned roles, a plan exists to address gaps or risks.
